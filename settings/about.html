About

Manage Your Balboa Spa with the Homey App

This app is built for controlling Balboa hot tubs using the Balboa Wi-Fi Module 50350 with LAN connection. The app does not support cloud-connected Balboa spas.

Use the Homey app to manage and control your Balboa-Spa devices. Ensure that your Homey is on the same LAN as the hot tub.
